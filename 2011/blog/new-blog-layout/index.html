Dev Blog New Layout! (And Status Update…)

Written on September 6, 2011 by nantas in Blog

aBit Games Dev Blog is back! And say goodbye to our beautiful old theme

It still looks beautiful but it turned out to be too heavy for a dev blog. Me and Karas had a discussion about it earlier today and we agreed that when you start a new blog from scratch it’s really difficult to choose a simple layout because that will look empty as hell.

Now we got at least Super Sheep Tap and ex2D in our belt and a lot more to talk about. We want something simple, elegant and more eye ball friendly for developer readers. We will create individual product page for each of our game. If you are a happy user of our game, you can go to our Facebook pageÂ to communicate with us and get the latest update of our games there.

So much about the new face of dev blog. Now it’s a short status update:

We are working in parallel on ex2D and a secret new project (a puzzle game soon to be announced!). ex2D got a lot of exciting new features recently, I will have a compiled post about the discussion we have on Unity forum. For the new project, we are busy working on the prototype that will be released to public in a couple of weeks. Please be patient when we announce the title it will be playable already.

Also more concept art and showcase will come soon! Stay tuned!

Please tell us what you think about the new blog theme, what else do you want to see?
