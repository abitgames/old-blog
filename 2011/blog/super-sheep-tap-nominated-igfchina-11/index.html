Super Sheep Tap Is Nominated As Finalist Of IGF China 2011!

Written on September 28, 2011 by nantas in Blog

We have some exciting news this week. We submitted Super Sheep Tap into the competition of Indiependent Games Festival China 2011 earlier in August. And IGF China just announced the finalists for main competition and student competition yesterday. Super Sheep Tap is in there!

Super Sheep Tap will compete with other excellent titles such as One Tap Hero (by Coconut Island Studio) and Billy Makin Kid!  (by SLAB Games) for several awards! Check out all the videos of main competition and student competition finalists in this report on Gamasutra.

The IGF China main event and awards ceremony will take place on November 12, 2011 alongside GDC China. Karas and Summercards will be there. Wish us good luck!

However IGF China competition is not the only competition we care about. Recently I’ve been checking the Super Sheep Tap global leaderboards (powered by OpenFeint) a lot. And the competition in Arcade Highscore is totally out of control! Let me show you this screenshot from 3 days ago:

First place 854130, are you kidding me? To give you some reference, Karas holds the best record among aBit Games developers. And his highest score is 240300. We’ve been playing this game for millions of times and we didn’t think there’s much room for improvement from that mark.

Usually beginner player gets somewhere around 2000-8000 in this 60 seconds arcade mode. Thanks to the simple but various combo system. The possibility to get a higher score is infinite. And we are really glad that those players on the leaderboard love the game and put in so much time playing it. That’s the best thing that could happen for a game developer!

So a big big THANK YOU to all of our supporters! This is a great week for us and we will keep working hard to bring more and better games in the future! Don’t forget our next project will be announced soon

Let me end this post with another screenshot of Super Sheep Tap leaderboards. This time is the Classic Mode Most Tagged Sheep leaderboard. In this game mode, you can keep tagging sheep forever if you don’t make any mistake:

That’s it, almost 8000 sheep counted and tagged. My personal record is 500-ish. Big shoutout to Nicole1211, I hope you have a good sleep every night xD
