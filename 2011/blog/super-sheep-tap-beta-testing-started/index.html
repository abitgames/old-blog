Super Sheep Tap Beta Testing Started!

Written on June 19, 2011 by nantas in Blog

Our first game Super Sheep Tap (aka iCountSheep back in the days) is almost finished with development. The beta testing has started and it’s debugging time!

Super Sheep Tap is an iPhone game developed in Unity. It’s a casual game easy to pick up for everybody but still very challenging to get higher scores.

In the game, cute sheep are constantly coming across the screen, jumping over the fence in the middle. You need to tap anywhere on the screen to tag the jumping sheep. If the sheep come in pair, you can tap with two fingers to tag them both at the same time! The control is super easy, but the challenging part comes when the sheep getting faster and coming in flocks! Then you need to read how many sheep are coming in very little time, then make the correct number of taps using different number of fingers! It’s a exciting challenge for both your mind and fingers! Stay calm, stay swift and stay hyped!

Features:

  • Bright and cute graphics with detailed sprite animations.
  • Multitouch to make tagging flocks of sheep easy, and you can go free style about your tapping!
  • Two game modes to fit your preference, Classic mode is casual and relaxing, Arcade mode is fast paced and super challenging!
  • Excellent original music.
  • Different combos you can get by adding a little bit precise timing for your taps!
  • OpenFeint leaderboards and achievements. Be the best among your friends!
  • Tutorial and help provided, just in case
  • History statistic shows your progression of the game.

 

Now it’s a perfect time for you, my dear readers, to participate in our beta testing! You will:
