Super Sheep Tap is a cute rhythm game in a completely new fashion. You tap anywhere on the screen to tag sheep jumping over the fence.

As you keep tagging correctly, sheep will come in faster rhythm and come in flocks! Get your fingers ready for different situations, they are gonna dance on your screen!

Cute Graphic, Lovely Music
Relax yourself and enjoy some happy rhythm action with cute sheep and lovely music!

Simple Control For Everybody
Tap anywhere on screen when sheep are jumping, that's all you need to do. Full multitouch support lets you handle flocks of sheep easier!

Two Modes To Fit Your Style
Classic Mode is very relaxing and easy to pick up. But you can't afford making too many mistakes. Arcade Mode is fast paced action within 60 seconds, and Super Sheep included for some crazy action!

OpenFeint Support
Compare and challenge the high score of your friends! Play with style to get all the challenging achievements!

  • Device Compatibility: iPhone3GS, iPhone4, iPodTouch3G/4G
  • OS: iOS3.2 or higher
